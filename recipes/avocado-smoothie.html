Avocado Smoothie

This delicious green smoothie is both healthy and filling - perfect for an in-between meal snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) orange juice
  • 1 ripe medium Hass avocado (about 6 ounces; 170 g)
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen mango (about 8 ounces; 227 g)
  • 1 large ripe banana (about 6 ounces; 170 g)
  • 1 packed cup (40 g) baby spinach, washed well of any sand or grit

Directions

  1. Combine orange juice, avocado, frozen mango, banana, and spinach in a blender.
  2. Pulse a few times to break up the fruit, then blend on high until completely smooth, about 1 minute.
